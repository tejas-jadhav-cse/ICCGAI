Frequently Asked Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Find answers to common questions about registration, submissions, venue, and conference logistics.

Registration & Attendance

What does registration include?

Registration includes access to all sessions, keynotes, workshops, conference materials, tea/coffee breaks, networking sessions, and digital proceedings. Lunch is available for purchase on campus.

Are there group discounts available?

Yes! Groups of 10+ attendees receive a 15% discount. Students from the same institution get 20% off for groups of 5+. Contact us for custom pricing for larger groups.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 15 days before the event. Partial refunds (50%) available up to 7 days before. No refunds within 7 days, but tickets are transferable to colleagues.

Do I need a student ID for student pricing?

Yes, valid student ID or enrollment verification is required. This can be uploaded during registration or presented at check-in. Student pricing is available for undergraduate, graduate, and PhD students.

Is there virtual attendance option?

This is an in-person event only. However, keynote sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees within 48 hours post-conference.

Papers & Presentations

What paper formats are accepted?

We accept full papers (6-8 pages), short papers (4 pages), poster papers (2 pages), and industry track submissions. All must follow IEEE conference format template.

Is the review process double-blind?

Yes, all submissions undergo double-blind peer review by at least 3 reviewers from our international program committee. Author identities are hidden during review.

Can I submit multiple papers?

Yes, but each paper must present substantially different research. Authors can submit up to 3 papers maximum. Each accepted paper requires at least one paid registration.

Will proceedings be indexed?

Yes, accepted papers will be published in IEEE Xplore and submitted for Scopus indexing. All papers will be available in Google Scholar and DBLP immediately after publication.

What if my paper is not accepted?

You'll receive detailed reviewer feedback. We encourage resubmission to future conferences. You can still attend as a regular participant and may be eligible for poster presentation of ongoing work.

Logistics & Travel

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, free parking is available on campus. Additional overflow parking is available within walking distance. We recommend carpooling when possible.

Are meals provided?

Tea, coffee, and light refreshments are included. Lunch can be purchased at the campus cafeteria (₹200-300) or nearby restaurants. We'll provide a list of recommended dining options.

What should I bring to the conference?

Bring a valid photo ID, your registration confirmation, business cards for networking, a laptop/tablet for notes, and comfortable walking shoes. Power outlets will be available in session rooms.

Is the venue accessible?

Yes, Sandip University is fully accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms. Please mention any special requirements during registration so we can assist you better.

Technical & Miscellaneous

Will WiFi be available?

Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi will be available throughout the conference venue. Network credentials will be provided during registration check-in.

Can I change my registration type?

Yes, registration type can be modified up to 7 days before the event. Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly. Contact us at ic-cigai@sandipuniversity.edu.in

Are presentation slides available?

Speakers may choose to share their slides. These will be made available to registered attendees through our conference portal within one week after the event.

What's the dress code?

Business casual is recommended. The venue is air-conditioned, so consider bringing a light jacket. For presentations, business attire is suggested but not mandatory.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, digital certificates of participation will be emailed within 5 business days. Paper certificates for presentations and workshops can be collected at the registration desk.
